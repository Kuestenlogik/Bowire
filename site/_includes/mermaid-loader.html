blocks. The loader is a no-op when no diagram is present, so it is cheap to include site-wide. Why client-side and not build-time SVG: build-time pre-rendering needs a headless Chromium in CI (mermaid-cli pulls it in) which doubles the docs workflow runtime. Lazy-loading the runtime keeps the CI cost zero and the page weight zero on pages without diagrams. Theming: we don't pick one of Mermaid's bundled themes — they fight our own palette tokens. Instead, theme: 'base' + themeVariables read from computed --accent / --surface / --text-primary on , so the diagram always matches the active theme exactly. main.js dispatches a 'themechange' CustomEvent on cycle; we listen and re-render.
